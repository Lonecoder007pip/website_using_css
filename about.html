About

At b&t, we believe that creativity should be limitless, unbound by financial constraints or accessibility challenges. That’s why we are dedicated to curating and publishing stunning, high-quality images that are entirely free for personal use. Whether you're working on a personal project, enhancing a blog, designing social media posts, or simply looking for inspiration, our extensive collection is here to support your vision.

Our Mission

b&t exists to bridge the gap between creativity and accessibility. We understand that not everyone has the resources to purchase licensed images, and we believe that beautiful visuals should be available to all—without restrictions. Our goal is to empower individuals to express themselves freely, using our images as a canvas for their ideas, stories, and passions.

What We Offer

Our platform provides an ever-expanding library of diverse, high-resolution images covering a wide range of subjects—from nature and architecture to abstract art and everyday life. Every image is carefully selected to ensure quality and relevance, helping users bring their projects to life without worrying about costs or complex licensing agreements.

Why Free?

We believe that access to beautiful visuals should not be limited by price tags. By offering images for free, we encourage personal creativity, independent projects, and the exchange of ideas without financial barriers. Our commitment to open access means that anyone, anywhere, can benefit from our collection without restrictions.

How We Sustain Our Work

As a nonprofit, our ability to provide free images is supported by the generosity of donors, sponsors, and volunteers who believe in our mission. We also collaborate with photographers and artists who share their work with the world through b&t, fostering a community of creators who want their art to inspire others. By working together, we ensure that our platform remains a valuable resource for personal use.

Join Our Movement

b&T is more than just a collection of images—it’s a movement. A movement toward creativity without boundaries, visual storytelling without limitations, and artistic expression without financial constraints. We invite you to explore our library, use our images in your projects, and spread the word about our mission to make visuals freely accessible to all
